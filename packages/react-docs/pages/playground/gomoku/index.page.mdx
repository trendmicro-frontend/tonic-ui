# Gomoku

## Overview

Gomoku, also known as Five in a Row, is a strategy board game played by two players on a 15x15 grid. The objective is to be the first player to form an unbroken line of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Players take turns placing their pieces—traditionally black and white—on the intersections of the board. Unlike Othello, captured pieces do not change color, and the game ends as soon as a player achieves five in a row. Gomoku is simple to learn but requires deep strategic thinking, making it a popular game in both casual and competitive settings.
